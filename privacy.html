Privacy Policy

Last updated January 2026.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly — such as name, email, phone number, employment details, and rental history — when you submit a rental application or create a landlord account. We also collect usage data like pages visited and actions taken on our platform.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process rental applications and communicate with applicants and landlords
  • To run background and credit checks (with your explicit consent)
  • To send status updates and notifications related to your application
  • To improve our platform and services

Information Sharing

We share application information with the landlord of the property you applied for. We share necessary information with our background check provider. We do not sell your personal information to third parties or use it for advertising.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure infrastructure (Supabase with row-level security) to protect your data. Sensitive fields like Social Security Numbers are never stored in browser storage.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at support@choiceproperties.com.
